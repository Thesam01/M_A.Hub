Market Commentary
Q4 2024 M&A Outlook

Global M&A activity is accelerating into Q4 2024 as interest rate stabilisation unlocks deal financing. Technology and energy consolidation remain the dominant themes, with AI infrastructure and critical minerals driving the largest transactions. Regulatory scrutiny from the DOJ and EU continues to extend timelines for cross-border deals, particularly in technology.

2025 Deal Forecast
Projected $4.5T Global Volume

Analysts project global M&A volume to reach $4.5T in 2025, driven by PE buyout acceleration ($3.9T dry powder), strategic technology consolidation, and energy transition M&A. Healthcare and AI infrastructure are expected to lead deal count growth, with average deal sizes expanding as mega-deals return to pre-2022 levels.
